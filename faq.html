Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I open an account?
A: You can open an account by visiting any of our branches with the required documents.
Q: What are the requirements for opening an account?
A: You will need to provide a valid ID, proof of address, and an initial deposit.
Q: How can I transfer money to another account?
A: You can transfer money online through our internet banking portal or visit a branch for assistance.
Q: What is the minimum balance requirement for a savings account?
A: The minimum balance requirement for a savings account is $100.
Q: How do I report a lost or stolen card?
A: You can report a lost or stolen card by calling our customer service hotline immediately.
Q: Can I apply for a loan online?
A: Yes, you can apply for a loan online through our website.
